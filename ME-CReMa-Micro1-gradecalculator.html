AM and AF are Weighted Geometric Means. The max() terms ensure Intro to Micro Theory exams can only help, never hurt, your midterm and final grades. If F < 4, the global grade is capped at 4.8: G = min(max(0.5·AM + 0.5·AF, AF), 4.8). If F ≥ 4: G = max(0.5·AM + 0.5·AF, AF). A grade of 0 in the Midterm Exam or the Final Exam collapses the adjusted grades, AM and AF, to 0, regardless of the grades IMTA and IMTB.
